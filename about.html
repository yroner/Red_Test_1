Mission Statement

Red Letter AI is one of the few companies at the cutting edge of generative political copy-writing - and currently the only one that caters exclusively to conservative organizations and campaigns.

While larger-scale initiatives have the spare resources to keep dedicated copy-writers on retainer to write political material, across the West there are thousands of conservative candidates running for local or municipal offices that have to triage resources and labor, and are forced to busy staffers with tedious copy-writing work instead of canvassing or engaging with voters where it really matters.

Every campaign relies on fundraising and other types of copy-writing to perform outreach and raise capital, unfortunately, the traditional methods of political copy-writing are both time and labor-intensive - often taking hours to move through the drafting, feedback, revision, and approval stages. To make matters worse, the often quite targeted and hyper-specific content makes such copy-writing inflexible and difficult to repurpose.

That's where Red Letter AI comes in - our goal is to provide conservative organizations with the ability to produce high quality, personalized, and tailored copy-writing material at the press of a button, eliminating the bottlenecks associated with traditional copy-writing.

Simply by spending a short amount of time filling out your campaign's profile and background on our platform, Red Letter AI's LLM-powered product is able to call on this information and more to generate compelling copy-writing material that is able to take into account the nuances of your mission and intelligently communicate this to any audience, streamlining your outreach strategies.

Conservatives have always had to do more with less - make sure you do it the smart way.
